A daily path into the heart.

The Archetypal Masculine Heart Quest brings together three streams of work into one guided progression: opening the heart, learning the language of story and symbol, and planting deeper intentions into the heart and subconscious.

  • short video lessons
  • reflection prompts and integration questions
  • completion-based 24-hour unlock pacing
  • optional reminder support by email or text

The quest unfolds in three arcs.

Each arc deepens the work in a different way: emotional honesty, symbolic/archetypal understanding, and intentional planting for the path ahead.

Days 1–7

Unlock the Heart

A return to feeling, honesty, and the emotional truths that shape life beneath the surface.

Days 8–14

Language of the Heart

A journey through Iron John, symbol, story, identity, shadow, and the deeper architecture of masculinity.

Days 15–21

Intentions Worth Planting

Planting qualities into the heart and subconscious so the quest continues in lived form.
