My Story

Aloha from the Big Island of Hawaii.

My name is Paul, or Pauko on the Island. I bring Emotional Alchemy, Heart Science, and both ancient and modern tools for health and wellness so people can live more fully.

Schedule A CallOpen Text Map

Story section 1

I knew things were out of balance because...

  • The heart, body, and mind were not yet living in honest relationship.
  • There was a need to understand healing in a more whole way.
  • Modern life and modern healthcare were not offering the full answer.
  • The deeper path required going within as well as caring for physical life.

Story section 2

The personal struggle

From Paul's work and research came the conviction that the heart is not symbolic alone, but central to our emotional and energetic life. The deeper realization was that healing with the heart is not separate from physical health or mental clarity — it is one of the keys to both.

This story section still needs more of Paul's personal history, but the direction is now aligned with the real message already present on the live coaching site.

Story section 3

Asking for help, rebuilding, integrating

Part of this work is supporting people in going within. Part of it is supporting physical and exterior health as well. That bridge between inner and outer healing is what shapes Paul's way of guiding others.

Wherever you are on your path, you do not have to walk it alone.
